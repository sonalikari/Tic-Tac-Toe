TIC-TAC-TOE

Play with A Friend or play with BOT.

Tic-tac-toe game(also called Noughts and Crosses) is a game in which two players take turns in drawing either an ' O' or an ' X' in one square of a grid consisting of nine squares. The winner is the first player to get three of the same symbols in a row.
